How Trézór Bridge® Works

The bridge operates on a multi-layer verification system that ensures complete transaction integrity from origin to destination chain. When you initiate a transfer:

  1. Assets are temporarily escrowed in our secure, audited smart contracts
  2. Multiple validator nodes verify the transaction request
  3. Once consensus is reached, assets are minted or released on the destination chain
  4. All steps are recorded immutably on both chains for complete transparency

This process typically completes in 2-5 minutes, depending on network congestion, with fees significantly lower than traditional centralized exchanges.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which blockchains does Trézór Bridge® support? +

Currently, Trézór Bridge® supports Ethereum, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Avalanche, and Arbitrum, with plans to add Solana, Polkadot, and Cosmos support in Q3 2023. Our roadmap includes adding 3-5 new chains quarterly based on community voting.

How does security compare to other bridges? +

Trézór Bridge® implements several security innovations not found in other bridges: military-grade encryption for all cross-chain communication, multi-sig validator requirements, time-delayed withdrawals for large transactions, and optional 2FA verification. Our smart contracts undergo monthly audits by third-party security firms.

What fees are involved in bridging? +

Trézór Bridge® charges a flat 0.1% fee (minimum $5 equivalent) per transfer, plus the gas/network fees on both chains. Premium members (holding 5000+ TZR tokens) receive 50% off bridging fees and priority processing during high network congestion.

Can I bridge NFTs using Trézór Bridge®? +

Yes! Our NFT bridging support launched in January 2023 and currently supports ERC-721 and ERC-1155 tokens between Ethereum, Polygon, and BSC. Each NFT collection requires individual approval by our security team to prevent fraudulent assets. The verification process typically takes 3-5 business days.
